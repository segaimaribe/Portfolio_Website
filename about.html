Biography

I am Segai Bryton Mampshika, a passionate and motivated Information Technology graduate from Tshwane University of Technology. With a foundation in Information Systems, Business Analysis, and Software Development, I have continuously strengthened my expertise through a blend of formal education, technical certifications, and hands-on work experience across both the private and public sectors. Throughout my journey, I've built practical skills in SQL, HTML/CSS, Visual Basics, SQL Server, and C#, complemented by strong experience in data administration, software troubleshooting, and digital systems support.

My roles at organizations like WITS VIDA, Department of Basic Education, and ABinBev have sharpened my data accuracy, attention to detail, and understanding of professional digital environments. Beyond technical expertise, I bring excellent communication, teamwork, and problem-solving skills, refined through structured development programs such as the CAPACITI Digital Career Accelerator, Transcend's Realise Programme, and Afrika Tikkun's Work Readiness Programme.

Career Objective

To contribute to impactful AI/ML projects in a forward-thinking organization, using my skills in deep learning, NLP, and data engineering to drive innovation and business value.
